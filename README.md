# pizza-sales-analysis-sql-powerbi
End-to-end data analysis project exploring pizza sales performance using SQL and Power BI. Including data cleaning, business KPI analysis, and a interactive dashboard revealing revenue trends, customer ordering behaviour, and product performance insight.

Technical Report on (SQL + Power BI) Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard 
For The Year 2024
Project Overview
This project analyzes a pizza sales dataset sourced from Kaggle to uncover business insights that can support decision-making in a food service business.
Using SQL for data exploration and transformation and Power BI for visualization, the project identifies key sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance.
The final output is an interactive business intelligence dashboard that tracks revenue performance, sales distribution, and product popularity.
Business Objectives
•	The primary objectives of this project include:
•	Analyze overall pizza sales performance
•	Identify best and worst selling pizzas
•	Understand customer ordering patterns
•	Examine sales trends by day and month
•	Determine which pizza sizes and categories contribute most to revenue
•	Provide data-driven recommendations for business improvement
Project Workflow
The analysis followed a structured data analytics process.
1️. Data Collection
The pizza sales dataset was obtained from Kaggle.
2️. Data Cleaning (SQL)
Data preparation steps included:
Removing duplicate records
Standardizing date and time formats
Validating numerical values
Creating calculated fields
3️. Data Exploration (SQL)
SQL queries were used to answer key business questions such as:
1.	Total revenue generated
2.	Total pizzas sold
3.	Total number of orders
4.	Average order value
5.	Average pizzas per order
6.	Best and worst selling pizzas
7.	Sales by category and size
8.	Order trends over time
 Data Visualization (Power BI)
The cleaned dataset was imported into Power BI to build an interactive dashboard containing:
1.	KPI summary cards
2.	Daily order trends
3.	Monthly sales trends
4.	Category and size distribution
5.	Top and bottom performing pizzas

Dashboard Insights
1.	Sales Trend Analysis
	Daily order trends show that:
	Weekend orders are significantly higher
	Friday and Saturday are the busiest days
	Monthly trends reveal that:
	July recorded the highest order volume
	September and October experienced lower sales activity
2.	Sales Distribution by Pizza Category
Sales were distributed across four major categories:
	Classic
	Supreme
	Veggie
	Chicken
Insight
The Classic category contributes the highest share of total sales of 26.91%, making it the most popular pizza type.
3.	Sales Distribution by Pizza Size
Customer preference strongly favors Large pizzas, which contribute the highest percentage of total sales of 45.89%
Key takeaway
Customers often prefer larger pizzas for group orders or sharing.
4.	Best Selling Pizzas
Top pizzas by revenue include:
	Thai Chicken Pizza 
	Barbecue Chicken Pizza
	California Chicken Pizza
	Classic Deluxe Pizza
These pizzas consistently drive the majority of revenue.
5.	Worst Performing Pizzas
	Low-performing pizzas include:
	Brie Carre Pizza
	Mediterranean Pizza
	Spinach Pizza
These products generate the lowest revenue and order volumes.
 Business Insights
I.	Several important insights were identified during the analysis:
II.	Sales are highest on weekends, indicating leisure-time demand.
III.	Large pizzas dominate sales, showing a strong preference for shareable meals.
IV.	A small number of pizzas drive the majority of revenue.
V.	Some menu items consistently underperform, suggesting low customer demand.
Business Recommendations
I.	Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:
II.	Promote Top-Selling Products
III.	Highlight best-selling pizzas in marketing campaigns and promotional offers.
IV.	Improve or Replace Underperforming Products
V.	Low-performing menu items may require:
VI.	Recipe improvement
VII.	Better menu placement
VIII.	Promotional discounts
IX.	Weekend Promotions
X.	Introduce targeted offers such as:
XI.	Weekend family deals
XII.	Combo meal promotions
XIII.	Inventory Optimization
XIV.	Ensure sufficient stock of ingredients used in high-demand pizzas.
